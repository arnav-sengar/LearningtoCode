Constants
-----------------
They are very similar to C++ variables
    have names
    occupy storage
    are usually typed

**HOWEVER, THEIR VALUE CANNOT BE CHANGED ONCE DECLARED!

Types of Constants in C++
------------------
    Literal constants 
    Declared constants
        const keyword
    Constant expressions
        constexpr keyword
    Enumerated constants
        enum keyword
    Defined constants
        #define
